Liquid filling machine

Description

  • Design of a Liquid Filling Machine based on specification provided by clients.
  • Determine design parameters based on the customers needs.
  • The design is later on modified for filling medicinal vials.

How?

  • 3D Model designed by Solidworks 2019.
  • Structural simulation performed on ANSYS.
  • The design went through various iteration to obtain optimum results

Results

  • The machine satisfied the customers meeting major constraints.
  • Our team came accross the problem of matching the size of the filler pistons with the vials.
  • Later on used a star arrangement to solve size incongruence between the pistons and various vial sizes.
